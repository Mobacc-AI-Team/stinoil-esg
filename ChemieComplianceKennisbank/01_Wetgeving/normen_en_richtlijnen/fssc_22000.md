 How FSSC benefits your organization 
 
 The Foundation FSSC provides oversight, transparency, and industry involvement to ensure our Schemes remain relevant and credible within the consumer goods industry. 
 
 
 All benefits 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Non-Profit 
 Foundation FSSC is a not-for-profit organization with an independent governance structure. Giving back is key to FSSC&#8217;s strategy. As part of our giving back strategy, we support UN capacity-building activities. 
 
 Read more 
 
 
 
 
 
 Providing trust 
 Our Schemes are governed by an independent Board of Stakeholders. Through the FSSC Integrity Program we monitor compliance of our licensed Certification Bodies, to ensure a robust and trusted certification process. 
 
 Read more 
 
 
 
 
 
 Risk-based approach 
 Our Schemes are aligned with the ISO management system approach that allows for flexibility in implementation, based on a risk-based approach. 
 
 Read more 
 
 
 
 
 
 Supporting SMEs 
 We support small and medium-sized organizations (SMEs) through a process of knowledge-sharing and other tools to further build an understanding of food safety and social sustainability. One of our tools is the FSSC 22000 Development Program. 
 
 Read more 
 
 
 
 
 
 GFSI and SSCI recognition 
 Our FSSC 22000 Scheme holds the Consumer Goods Forum’s (CGF) Global Food Safety Initiative (GFSI) recognition, and is IAF-approved. The FSSC 24000 Scheme for social management systems is recognized by the CGF’s Social Supply Chain Initiative (SSCI), endorsed as an IAF MLA sub-scope, and part of the ITC Standards Map. All recognitions confirm i ndustry acceptance and technical rigor .

 Contributing to SDGs 
 As a UN Global Compact member, we aim to actively contribute to the Sustainable Development Goals.

 Assuring trust via the 
 
 FSSC Integrity Program 
 
 FSSC provides trust through its multi-stakeholder governance, partnering with recognized Accreditation Bodies and the deployment of its Integrity Program, assuring the acceptable performance of licensed Certification Bodies. 
 The FSSC Integrity Program ensures Certification Body compliance with Scheme requirements through assessment activities and performance measurement resulting in trusted certification. 
 
 Learn more 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 

 
 
 
 
 
 Take your first step toward food safety certification 
 
 FSSC Development Program 
 
 Small and medium-sized organizations (SMEs) play a significant role in the food supply chain. To support these organizations in the supply of safe food, we offer the FSSC Development Program . The FSSC Development Program is aligned with CODEX HACCP, GFSI Global Markets, and fits into the FSSC 22000 structure. From a procurement perspective, it serves as a tool to assure food safety in your supply base.
